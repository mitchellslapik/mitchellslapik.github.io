I am currently an MD/PhD student in the Medical Scientist Training Program at McGovern Medical School. I study computational neuroscience in Valentin Dragoi's lab, where I focus on the visual cortex. Previously, I did research on the cerebellum in Cherie Marvel's lab at Johns Hopkins University, and studied philosophy at Swarthmore College and University of Oxford. Ultimately, I plan to specialize in psychiatry and use machine learning to better understand and treat psychiatric disorders. Outside of work, I volunteer at Crisis Text Line and play jazz saxophone in The Chirp Chirps and Bayou City Funk.

For more information, see my CV or Google Scholar.
