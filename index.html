McGovern Medical School, University of Texas at Houston

I am an MD/PhD candidate in the Medical Physican Scientist Training Program at McGovern Medical School. I am currently purusing my PhD in Neuroscience in Dr. Valentin Dragoi's lab, using computational methods to study the visual cortex. Previously, I did research in Dr. Cherie Marvel's lab at Johns Hopkins University where I studied the cognitive effects of cerebellar ataxia. I studied philosophy and linguistics in the Honors Program at Swarthmore College and University of Oxford.

In addition to my research, I play jazz saxophone in The Chirp Chirps and volunteer at the Crisis Text Line. For additional information, see my Curriculum Vitae or Google Scholar.
